About

FFPDX is a family-oriented business that offers a fun and unique experience for children, teens and adults of all ages. Our services incorporate the art of hula-hooping, poi-spinning and other flow arts. The goal of Family Flow PDX is to inspire your whole family to have fun while staying active and to find the flow in your family.
